Chocolate Cake

About This Recipe

Brownie is a dense, fudgy chocolate delight with a crackly top and a gooey center. Baked to perfection, it offers the perfect balance of bittersweet cocoa and buttery richness. Best enjoyed warm, often paired with a scoop of vanilla ice cream or a drizzle of chocolate sauce.

Ingredients

  • ½ cup Butter (melted) or ¼ cup Oil
  • 1 cup Sugar
  • 2 Eggs (or flax eggs for vegan)
  • 1 tsp Vanilla Extract
  • ⅓ cup Cocoa Powder
  • ½ cup All-Purpose Flour
  • ¼ tsp Salt
  • ¼ tsp Baking Powder
  • ½ cup Chocolate Chips/Walnuts (optional)

Instructions

  1. Prep: Preheat oven to 175°C (350°F). Grease an 8x8-inch pan.
  2. Wet Mix: Whisk melted butter, sugar, eggs, and vanilla until smooth.
  3. Dry Mix: Sift cocoa, flour, salt, and baking powder. Fold into wet mix gently.
  4. Add-Ins: Stir in chocolate chips/nuts (if using).
  5. Bake: Spread batter in pan. Bake 20-25 mins (toothpick should have moist crumbs).
  6. Cool: Let cool in pan for 10 mins before slicing.

Tips

  • Fudgy vs. Cakey: Reduce flour to ⅓ cup for fudgier texture.
  • Double Chocolate: Add ¼ cup melted chocolate to the batter.
  • Storage: Keep airtight for 3 days or freeze for 1 month.
