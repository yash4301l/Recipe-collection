Butter Chicken

About This Recipe

Butter Chicken, also known as Murgh Makhani, is a creamy and mildly spiced North Indian curry made with tender, marinated chicken simmered in a velvety tomato-butter sauce. Its rich flavor, balanced by hints of fenugreek and cream, makes it a favorite with naan or steamed basmati rice.

Ingredients

  • 500g Chicken (boneless, cubed)
  • ½ cup Yogurt
  • 1 tbsp Lemon Juice
  • 1 tbsp Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • 1 tsp Red Chili Powder
  • ½ tsp Turmeric Powder
  • 1 tsp Garam Masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp Butter
  • 1 tbsp Oil
  • 1 Onion (finely chopped)
  • 1 cup Tomato Puree
  • 1 tsp Kasuri Methi (dried fenugreek leaves)
  • ½ cup Fresh Cream
  • 1 tbsp Honey/Sugar (optional)
  • ¼ cup Water (as needed)
  • Coriander Leaves (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Marinate Chicken: Mix yogurt, lemon juice, ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, turmeric, garam masala, and salt. Coat chicken and refrigerate for 1 hour (or overnight).
  2. Cook Chicken: Grill or pan-fry marinated chicken until lightly charred. Set aside.
  3. Make Gravy: Heat butter and oil. Sauté onions until golden. Add tomato puree and cook until oil separates.
  4. Blend: Cool the mixture, then blend into a smooth paste.
  5. Simmer: Return paste to pan. Add kasuri methi, cream, honey, and water. Simmer for 5 minutes.
  6. Combine: Add cooked chicken. Simmer for 8-10 minutes on low heat.
  7. Serve: Garnish with cream and coriander. Enjoy with naan or rice!

Tips

  • Creamier Texture: Add 2 tbsp cashew paste with tomato puree.
  • Smoky Flavor: Use the "dhungar" method (hot charcoal + ghee) before serving.
  • Vegan Option: Replace chicken with tofu/paneer, and use coconut cream.
