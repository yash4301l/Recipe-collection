Chocolate Cake

About This Recipe

Chocolate Cake is a timeless dessert made with moist cocoa-rich sponge layers and luscious chocolate frosting. Its deep, indulgent flavor and soft crumb make it a favorite for celebrations or simple sweet cravings. Whether served warm or chilled, this classic treat never fails to satisfy.

Ingredients

  • 1¾ cups All-Purpose Flour
  • 2 cups Granulated Sugar
  • ¾ cup Cocoa Powder (unsweetened)
  • 1½ tsp Baking Powder
  • 1½ tsp Baking Soda
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 2 Eggs (at room temperature)
  • 1 cup Milk (whole or buttermilk)
  • ½ cup Vegetable Oil
  • 2 tsp Vanilla Extract
  • 1 cup Hot Water (or hot coffee for deeper flavor)

Instructions

  1. Prep: Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour two 9-inch round pans or line with parchment paper.
  2. Dry Mix: In a large bowl, sift together flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. Wet Mix: Add eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla to the dry ingredients. Mix on medium speed for 2 minutes.
  4. Add Liquid: Gradually stir in hot water (or coffee) until the batter is smooth (it will be thin).
  5. Bake: Pour batter evenly into prepared pans. Bake for 30-35 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.
  6. Cool: Let cakes cool in pans for 10 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely.
  7. Frost: Frost with chocolate ganache or buttercream once fully cooled.

Notes

  • Fluffy Texture: Do not overmix the batter after adding hot water.
  • Rich Flavor: Using hot coffee instead of water enhances chocolate taste.
  • Storage: Store covered at room temperature for up to 3 days or refrigerate for longer freshness.
