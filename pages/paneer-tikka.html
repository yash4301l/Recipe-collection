Paneer Tikka

About This Recipe

Paneer Tikka is a classic Indian appetizer made by marinating chunks of paneer in a spicy yogurt-based marinade, then grilled to smoky perfection. This vibrant, protein-packed vegetarian delight is a hit at every party or meal.

Ingredients

  • 200g Paneer, cubed
  • 1/2 cup Thick Yogurt
  • 1 tsp Red Chili Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Turmeric Powder
  • 1 tsp Garam Masala
  • 1 tbsp Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • 1 tbsp Lemon Juice
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tbsp Oil
  • 1 Onion & 1 Capsicum, cut in chunks

Instructions

  1. Marinate: Mix yogurt, spices, paste, lemon juice, salt, and oil. Add paneer and veggies, coat well, and rest for 30 minutes.
  2. Skewer: Thread paneer and veggies alternately onto skewers.
  3. Grill: Roast on a tawa, grill pan, or oven until slightly charred (about 10-15 minutes).
  4. Serve: Serve hot with green chutney and lemon wedges.
